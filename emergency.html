🆘 Sister Shield Emergency Mode

Immediate cyber safety response console for phishing, fake accounts, cyberbullying, photo misuse, impersonation, and suspicious online threats.

🔒 Secure Your Accounts

  • Change passwords immediately
  • Enable 2-factor authentication
  • Log out from all devices
  • Check recent login activity

📱 If Photos / Accounts Are Misused

  • Block the fake profile
  • Report impersonation on platform
  • Take screenshots as evidence
  • Inform trusted contact immediately

⚠ If It Was a Phishing Scam

  • Do not enter OTP or password
  • Check bank / UPI activity
  • Freeze risky sessions
  • Contact bank support if needed

🛡 Suggested Next Response

  • Report fake accounts
  • Review privacy settings
  • Restrict who can message / view
  • Save evidence before deleting anything

📞 India Cyber Safety Help
